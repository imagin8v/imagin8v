DESIGN FOR TRUST

Belong Anywhere

The Service

Hospitality is one of the society's most deeply-held cultural practices, rooted in centuries of tradition and beliefs among all cultures.

Building Community

Once trust takes root, powerful community effects begin to emerge and long-standing barriers can begin to fall. Rather than staying with other tourists, people stay with locals, creating an opportunity for cross-cultural connection that can break down barriers and increase understanding.

The Reputation System

Reviews becomes the trust factor for guests to choose the right host and builds reputation. Having any reputation at all is a strong determinant of a host’s ability to get a booking  - a host without reviews is about four times less likely to get a booking than a host that has at least one.
