Distribution

If You Build It, Will They Come

Long term value of Advertising

It's easy to resist the most obvious sales pitches, so we entertain a false confidence in our independence of mind. But advertising doesn't exist to make you buy a product right away; it exists to embed subtle impression that will drive sales later.

Viral products needs distribution too

No matter how strong your product - even if it easily fits into already established habits and anybody who tries it likes it immediately - you must still support it with a strong distribution plan.

Economics of Effective Distribution

Two metrics set the limits for effective distribution. The total net profit that you earn on average over the course of your relationship with a customer (Customer Lifetime Value) must exceed the amount you spend on average to acquire a new customer (Customer Acquisition Cost).

Selling to Non-Customers

Even if your particular product doesn't need media exposure to acquire customers because you have a viral distribution strategy, the press can help attract investors and employees.
